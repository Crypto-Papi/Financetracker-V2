# Accounts Section Setup Guide

The Accounts section displays your net worth, credit cards, loans, student loans, and other debts with a net worth trend chart.

## How Data is Organized

The app automatically categorizes your transactions into:
- **Credit Cards**: Transactions with "credit" or "card" in the category
- **Loans**: Transactions with "loan", "auto", or "vehicle" in the category
- **Student Loans**: Transactions with "student" in the category
- **Other Debts**: Transactions with "debt" in the category
- **Assets**: Income or asset type transactions

## Adding Accounts

### Via the Dashboard Form:

1. Go to the **Transactions** section
2. Fill in the form with:
   - **Description**: Name of the account (e.g., "Chase Sapphire Card")
   - **Amount**: Total balance or limit
   - **Type**: Select "expense" or "debt"
   - **Category**: Use keywords like:
     - "Credit Card" for credit cards
     - "Auto Loan" for car loans
     - "Student Loan" for student loans
     - "Personal Debt" for other debts
   - **Remaining Balance**: Current balance owed
   - **Interest Rate**: APR percentage (e.g., 18.5)
   - **Due Date**: Payment due date (optional)

3. Click "Add Transaction"

## Net Worth Calculation

Net Worth = Total Assets - Total Liabilities

The summary on the right shows:
- Total Assets (green)
- Breakdown of all liabilities
- Final Net Worth
